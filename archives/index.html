About me

I am Diandian Gu (谷 典典), a thrid-year CS Ph.D. candidate at Computer Systems Research Group, Peking University. I am currently advised by Prof. Xuanzhe Liu and Prof. Xin Jin. Previously, I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Peking University.

My research mainly focuses on systems for machine learning, distributed deep learning, and cluster resource management. I am also interested in software engineering.
