About me

I am Diandian Gu (谷典典), a first-year CS PhD student at Software Engineering Institute, Peking University. I am currently advised by Prof. Xuanzhe Liu. I am also working closely with Prof. Xin Jin. Previously, I received my CS Bachelor’s degree in Peking University.

My research mainly focuses on machine learning systems, distributed deep learning, and cluster resource management. I am also interested in software engineering.
